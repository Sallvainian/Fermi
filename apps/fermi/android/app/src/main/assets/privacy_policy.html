Privacy Policy for Fermi

Effective Date: January 17, 2025
Last Updated: January 17, 2025
Educational Platform: Fermi is designed for teachers and students. We comply with FERPA, COPPA, and GDPR to protect student privacy and educational records.

Who We Are

Academic Tools operates Fermi, an education management platform for teachers and students. We are committed to protecting your privacy, especially for minors using our platform.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Info: Name, email, username, profile photo
  • Student Data: Grade level, parent email, class enrollment
  • Educational Records: Assignments, grades, submissions, progress

Technical Information

  • Device Data: Device type, OS version, app version
  • Usage Data: App performance, error logs, session info
  • Security Data: Authentication tokens, login records

Communication Data

  • Messages: Chat messages, notifications
  • Calls: Voice/video call metadata (not recordings)
  • Files: Assignment uploads, profile pictures

How We Use Your Information

Educational Purposes

  • Providing educational services and class management
  • Tracking academic progress and creating reports
  • Facilitating teacher-student communication
  • Managing assignments and submissions

Account & Security

  • Creating and maintaining user accounts
  • Authenticating users securely
  • Providing customer support
  • Preventing fraud and abuse

Information Sharing

Educational Environment

  • Teachers can access their students' educational records
  • Students can access their own academic information
  • Parents can access their child's educational data

Service Providers

We use trusted services like Google Firebase for:

  • Secure authentication and database storage
  • Push notifications for educational activities
  • File storage for assignments and profiles
  • App performance monitoring (not user behavior)

What We DON'T Do

  • No Advertising: We don't show ads or track for advertising
  • No Selling: We never sell your personal information
  • No Behavioral Tracking: We don't track behavior across other apps/sites

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Age Restrictions

  • Students under 13 require parental consent
  • Teachers create accounts for students with permission
  • Limited data collection for minors
  • No advertising to children

Parental Rights

Parents can:

  • Review their child's information
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Refuse further data collection
  • Deactivate their child's account

Data Security

  • Encryption: Secure data transmission and storage
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to data
  • Authentication: Secure login with optional 2FA
  • Monitoring: Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Incident Response: Breach notification procedures

Your Rights

All Users

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account (subject to educational requirements)
  • Export your data
  • Control notification preferences

EU Users (GDPR)

  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to lodge complaints

Data Retention

  • Educational Records: Kept per FERPA requirements
  • Account Data: Deleted within 30 days of closure request
  • Technical Logs: 90 days for usage, 1 year for security
  • Communications: Duration of active use
